Impacts of Technologies in Our Daily Life

Introduction to Technology

Technology refers to methods, systems, and devices which are the result of scientific knowledge being used for practical purposes. The Internet might be the most significant social village that humanity has created in history. It is an informational resource that allows us to experience different perspectives, ideas, and cultures from all over the world. A popular saying that ‘There are two sides of a coin’ is also true in the case of Internet-technologies.There is pros and cons of technologies in our daile lives. We live in the age of technology where it has become an important part of our life but it can sometimes act as a hindrance too if dependency continues to increase.

Positive Impacts on People's Life

With the advancement in technology, the internet has become more intertwined with our lives than we can think of. Everything - from news to entertainment - is available at our fingertips with just a touch to a button or screen.In earlier times, communicating with a person belonging to a different part of the world was difficult. In the ancient time, people had to communicate using pigeons or letters that took days or weeks. However today the world has come closer and one can easily connect with a person sitting in another corner of the world. In this modern century, noone can imagine their life without the internet in their daily lives. Technology inspire us to embrace the inner nnovator. From computer design to farming, we are in the middle of another global revolution. New techniques on croplands can be used to increase yields without spreading pesticides. Layer farming let us create more food while using less space. This process let us raise livestock that is healthier, which leads to better products. Technology has developed better learning methods for many students and learners to learn new skills. Many Students refer google to learn and to implement many ideas on their work.Technology let us focus on thet ability instead of disability.One of Helen Keller’s most famous quotes is this: “The best and most beautiful things in the world cannot be seen or even touched – they must be felt with the heart.” Keller made history by becoming the first person who was deaf and blind to earn an undergraduate degree. She is known for her ability to overcome her disability through her personal desire to succeed. There are many technological options than ever before to help people reach their full potential.

Negative Impacts on People's Life

With each passing day, we are becoming more and more dependent on technology for almost every task without even realizing it. For example, Alot of people bother learning and they depend more on google for everything like for courseworks and using of calculator which reduces to perform mental calculations. Surfing and playing games on the Internet can quickly become very addictive. Spending alot of time on the Internet can hamper mentalhealth where it causes distractions and can lead to voilent activities.As with watching TV, spending too much time on the computer surfing the Internet or playing games can also lead to obesity and an unhealthy lifestyle.For example, moving your hand from your keyboard to a mouse and typing are all repetitive actions, which can cause injuries. Social sites can also lead to depression as many people tend to compare their lives with others.

Conclusion

Henceforth, we can see say that technology has both positive and negative impacts on our lives. The thing that matters is how we use the technology and for what we use the technology. All we can say is technology has brought a drastic changes in the lives of billions of people and it should not be used for evil purposes rather it should be used for the good of the mankind.
